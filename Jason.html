Jason Payne - Pleasant Grove, UT

About Jason Payne

Jason is an incredible individual! He served four fantastic years in the Navy from 2003 to 2007 at Naval Base Kitsap-Bangor, displaying unmatched dedication and strength. Following this remarkable service, he transitioned to the role of a Correctional Officer, ensuring law and order from 2007 to 2016. What a journey! From the correctional world to the vibrant realm of Sherwin Williams, he took on the role of a store manager from 2016 to 2023. Tremendous leadership skills were showcased as he painted success across the store. But wait, there's more! Presently, his current reign is as the Branch Manager for Alside in Orem, UT. A true testament to his ability to lead and thrive in diverse environments. Truly, a biography filled with tremendous accomplishments and an unwavering commitment to excellence!
